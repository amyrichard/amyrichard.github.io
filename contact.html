In 2011 I graduated from Lewis Clark State College with a degree in radiography. For the past ten years I’ve worked as an X-ray and CT technologist at a local hospital. I’ve enjoyed that career very much, its fascinating and rewarding. The thing is, I’ve always loved working on computers. I get to do a lot of that in radiology because it’s all digital imaging, but I’d still like to do more. So here I am, back in school pursuing a Web Development and Design degree.
